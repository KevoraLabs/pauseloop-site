Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

PauseLoop is designed as a private, local macOS app. It does not require an account and does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Data stored on your Mac

PauseLoop stores app preferences locally on your device, such as focus duration, break duration, reminder settings, sounds, schedule, and display options. This data remains on your Mac.

Notifications and system features

If you enable notifications, launch at login, Dock visibility, or display sleep prevention, PauseLoop uses the relevant macOS system capabilities only to provide those features. PauseLoop does not transmit this information to KevoraLabs.

Analytics and tracking

PauseLoop does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.
